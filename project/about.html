About Manwha Kicks

Manwha Kicks started as a place for fans to find honest reviews without spoilers ruining the read.

Our Story

What started as a personal list of recommendations turned into a full site once we realized how many ppl were looking for the same thing. We cover everything from massive hits like Solo Leveling to hidden gems most readers haven't heard of yet.

Our Mission

Help readers find their next favorite manhwa or manga. We focus on honest scoring, spoiler-free first impressions, and giving smaller series the spotlight they deserve.

How We Rate

  • Story - 40% of the score
  • Art - 30% of the score
  • Pacing - 20% of the score
  • Replay Value - 10% of the score

Every series gets a final score out of 10 along with a status tag (Ongoing, Completed, Hiatus).
