How Ratings Work

Every review on Manwha Kicks follows the same scoring system so you know exactly what each number means.

The Breakdown

  • Story (40%): Plot, characters, world-building, originality
  • Art (30%): Panel work, character design, action choreography
  • Pacing (20%): How well chapters flow, no filler or rushed arcs
  • Replay Value (10%): Is it worth a reread?

Score Meaning

  • 9.0 - 10: Must read, top tier
  • 8.0 - 8.9: Great, highly recommended
  • 7.0 - 7.9: Solid, worth your time
  • 6.0 - 6.9: Decent, has issues
  • Below 6: Skip unless you really like the genre
